The Apex Indicator by EY identifies high-probability gold (XAUUSD) trade setups using Fibonacci retracement levels and price action. Every trade is executed on a live IC Markets MT5 account and verified through Myfxbook — no screenshots, no cherry-picking. Connect your own Myfxbook account to track your performance alongside the community.

1

Open a Broker Account

Sign up for an IC Markets account (or use your existing broker). Choose a MetaTrader 5 (MT5) live or demo account.

Tip: IC Markets is recommended as EY's Apex Indicator is optimized for their spreads and execution. Standard or Raw Spread account types both work.
2

Create a Myfxbook Account

Go to myfxbook.com and create a free account. Myfxbook is a third-party platform that independently tracks and verifies your trading performance.

3

Connect Your MT5 to Myfxbook

In Myfxbook, go to Portfolio → Add Account. Select MetaTrader 5 (MT5) as your platform and choose AutoUpdate as the connection method. Enter your broker credentials when prompted.

Tip: AutoUpdate uses a read-only investor password — Myfxbook can only view your trades, never place or modify them. Your account is always safe.
4

Enable API Access

In Myfxbook, go to Settings → API Access and make sure it's turned ON. This allows the Trade Dashboard to securely pull your performance data.

5

Log In to the Trade Dashboard

Click "View Live Dashboard" above, then enter your Myfxbook email and password. Your equity curve, daily P&L, win rate, and full trade history will appear automatically.

Tip: The dashboard syncs in real time. Every trade you take will show up as soon as Myfxbook processes it — usually within minutes.
6

Join our free 2-hour webinar to learn how the Apex Indicator works, the strategy behind it, and how EY consistently trades gold with a 90%+ win rate. No experience required.
